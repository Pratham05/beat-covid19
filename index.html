What is Covid-19?

COVID-19 is an infection caused by the new coronavirus that is reported to be emerged in Wuhan, China in December 2019

The new coronavirus can be spread from person to person. It is diagnosed with a laboratory test

There is no coronavirus vaccine yet. The only way to deal with this disease for now is prevention

A lot of countries have gone into repetitive lockdowns to deal with this virus
